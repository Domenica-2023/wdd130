Overview

Purpose

This website will be about how to work as a video game designer. My dream job is to be a Video Game Designer. The purpose of this website will be to help me find my dream job and to help people like me in the future to find information to help them become a Game Designer too. My website will help people in the future know what to do and how to get their future job. My website will also serve as an informational website about me. It will include REAL customer reviews so that people will know what it is like to work with me. My website also will include information about my certifications, education, goals and dreams, professional education, and more. You will be able to book appointments on my website and my website will serve as a place of communication and collaboration among people interested in this line of work (video game design). Also if they can not meet up online (aka Zoom and etc.,) we can try and book an appointment whenever we are both available.

Audience

The target audience will be people interestedin working in Game Design or people interested in video games as a hobby. The audience's age would most likely be teenagers and older. It wouldn't be appropriate for children because they're not part of of the professional aspect of the website. This webite is for people who are wanting to study into this specific career or for people looking to make a career change. This website will be for people interested in working in Game Design, for people interested in building their skills, for people interested in this career professionally, and just for normal hobbyists. People visiting my website want information and to have a networking community. People will access my webste on any device they have access to.

Content

Home page

Hi, my name is Domenica and this page is about video game design. It is my dream to become a Video Game Designer one day. This is a website that will help people with the same dream as me to accomplish that. This website will offer community, resources and information for people interested in becoming game designers as well. This website will attempt to answer all your questions. People will learn information by watching videos about what it is like to work in video game design, by reading about the stuff that we do on a daily basis and by getting virtual tours around video game designer offices. My website will also offer a booking service to book in person tours at offices that allow visitors (prospective future game designers).
