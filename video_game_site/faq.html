FAQ

Who do I speak with to get a job in game design?

To get a job in game design, your best bet is to network. Actually, 85% of jobs are gotten through networking. It´s best to network and make sure your skills are up-to-date and ready to use at a moments notice. Here on our website, you can find ways to meet people and start networking now!

What credentials do I need to get a job in game design?

Some credentials you might need to enter a job as a game designer include: a computer science degree, coding experience and coding skills. The coding skills can be obtained through a 4 year degree program or also through a boot camp.

How do I begin to learn the skills that I will need in game design

You can start by enrolling in a boot camp. There are also a lot of free online resources to start learning coding. Here are some websites you can visit to get started on your computer science education…

www.codeacademy.com,www.freecodecamp.org, www.theodinproject.com, ocw.mit.edu, dash.generalassembly, dash.generalassemb.ly and www.classcentral.com

How much are game designers paid at first?

An entry level job won't be paid as much as someone that's worked in the field for many years. For an entry level job, you are looking at earning 50K.

How much are game designers paid when they are 10 and 15 years into their careers?

Game designers can be paid 100K and more when they are well into their careers. After some time learning how the field works, you can imagine that experienced game designers have a lot under their belt and can better navigate a lot of things. They have experience too which makes them very sought after for different high paying jobs. Your best bet is to get started learning and hopefully working ASAP!
